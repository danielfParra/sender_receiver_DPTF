The Berlin Social Science Center (Wissenschaftszentrum Berlin für Sozialforschung, WZB), Reichpietschufer 50, 10785 Berlin is conducting a scientific online study today. You have been recruited for this study because you have registered in the Prolific recruitment system. The procedures will be explained to you before the start of the study.

During the study you will face a number of different tasks and your responses will be recorded by our computer system. The data recorded in the study do not allow any inference about the participation or the decisions or responses of individual who participate in the study. Correspondingly, the analysis and presentation of all results of this study will be anonymized. The anonymous research data will be archived and will possibly be made available to other scientists for further use.

The data generated in the study will not be linked to the data in the Prolific recruitment system. The data collected for the payment of compensation (e-mail address or bank details) will always be managed by Prolific. We will have only access to your identifier, which we cannot link to your Prolific profile data. Participation in today‘s experiment is of course entirely voluntary. There will be no disadvantages for you if you decide not to participate. You have the possibility to exit the study at any time.

We want to ask for your consent to participate in the study and to the implied processing of data. You can revoke your consent at any time with an effect for the future, for example, by sending an e-mail to experimente@wzb.eu. Your consent is at the same time the legal basis for the processing of data.

For any further information about the processing of your data and data protection, especially about your rights to disclosure, correction, deletion, limitations of processing of data, objection, data transmission and complaints to a supervisory body, please contact the data protection officer of WZB: dataprotection@wzb.eu.

You cannot participate in today's experiment without consenting.
