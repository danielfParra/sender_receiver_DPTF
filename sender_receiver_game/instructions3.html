How does the matching of Player As and Player Bs work?

At the beginning of the experiment, you will be randomly allocated into groups of ten participants-five Player As and five Player Bs. In every round of the experiment, Player As and Player Bs are randomly re-matched into five pairs. This means that in any round, you can be matched with any Player B in your group.
